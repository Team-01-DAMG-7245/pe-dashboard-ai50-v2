## Company Overview
Photoroom is an AI-powered photo editing and listing studio designed for product photography. The company offers a suite of tools that enable users to create impactful product visuals quickly and efficiently, catering to individuals, small businesses, and enterprises alike. Their services include background removal, image generation, and product staging, among others.

## Business Model and GTM
Photoroom operates on a subscription-based model with various pricing tiers, starting from $7.50 per month for small businesses and creative teams, and $20.83 per month for larger enterprises. The company emphasizes automation and scalability in its offerings, allowing users to generate thousands of on-brand ads and optimize product listings for better sales performance. The company states that their tools can significantly reduce photo costs and improve visual quality without requiring design skills.

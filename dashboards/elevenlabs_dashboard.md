## Company Overview
ElevenLabs is a global leader in AI audio solutions, focusing on developing technologies that enhance voice capabilities across various applications. The company operates with a mission to shape the future of voice technology and AI, emphasizing responsible use and innovation.

## Business Model and GTM
ElevenLabs offers a range of products and services, including text-to-speech, speech-to-text, voice cloning, and AI music generation. The company targets various sectors such as media, education, and customer support, providing tailored solutions for enterprises, creators, and developers. The company states that it is building local talent hubs and infrastructure to deliver real-time AI audio globally.

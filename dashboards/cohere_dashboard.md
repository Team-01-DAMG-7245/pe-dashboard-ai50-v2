## Company Overview
Cohere is a private AI startup founded in 2019 in Toronto, Canada. The company builds foundational models and AI solutions aimed at enhancing human wellbeing by empowering businesses to scale innovation, boost productivity, and drive progress. Cohere has developed a range of products including an enterprise-ready AI platform called North, an intelligent search system named Compass, and a family of high-performance generative models called Command.

## Business Model and GTM
Cohere's business model focuses on providing AI solutions that integrate seamlessly into existing systems, enhancing workplace productivity and decision-making processes. The company offers various products tailored to different industries, including technology, financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, energy, and public sector. The company states that its AI solutions are designed for immediate impact and can be customized to fit specific use cases and infrastructure needs.

## Funding & Investor Profile
Cohere has secured four major funding rounds (Series A through D) between 2021 and 2024, raising nearly $1 billion. This funding has enabled the company to scale its teams, expand globally, and develop cutting-edge AI models.

## Growth Momentum
Since its founding, Cohere has experienced significant growth, particularly between 2021 and 2024. The launch of Cohere Labs in 2022, which focuses on solving complex machine learning problems, has attracted over 4,500 community members and resulted in the publication of over 100 research papers. The company continues to innovate with the launch of its AI platform, North, in 2025.

## Visibility & Market Sentiment
Cohere is recognized for its commitment to building secure and private AI solutions. The company states that its products are trusted by industry leaders and developers worldwide, indicating a positive market sentiment towards its offerings.

## Company Overview
ClearCaptions, LLC provides a captioning service that generates captions of what the other party to a phone call says. These captions are sent to the user's phone in near-real time. The service is designed for individuals with hearing loss and is available only in the United States and its territories.

## Business Model and GTM
ClearCaptions operates on a no-cost model for qualified users, where the service is funded through a federally administered Telecommunications Relay Services (TRS) fund. Individuals who qualify for the service do not incur any costs, as the expenses for caption generation are covered by this fund. The company states that it provides training and free setup for users, ensuring they can effectively use the captioning phone.

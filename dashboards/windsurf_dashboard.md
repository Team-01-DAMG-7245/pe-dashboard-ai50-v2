## Company Overview
Windsurf is a private AI startup focused on empowering software engineers by providing innovative tools that enhance coding efficiency and creativity. The company's mission is to help individuals and organizations reach their full potential in software development.

## Business Model and GTM
Windsurf operates on a freemium model, offering free access for individuals while providing enterprise solutions for organizations. The company emphasizes a seamless integration of its tools into popular IDEs, including JetBrains, to facilitate a smooth user experience.

## Funding & Investor Profile

## Growth Momentum
The company reports over 5 million plugin downloads and more than 100 million daily lines of code written using its platform, indicating strong user engagement and adoption.

## Company Overview
Speak is an AI-powered language learning app designed to facilitate real conversational practice without the need for a live tutor. The company aims to reinvent language learning by providing users with an interactive and personalized experience that helps them achieve fluency. Speak has quickly grown to become the #1 English app in South Korea and serves hundreds of thousands of subscribers across over 30 countries.

## Business Model and GTM
Speak operates on a subscription-based model, offering users access to its AI tutor, which provides personalized language learning experiences. The company emphasizes the importance of speaking out loud to learn a language effectively. Speak's go-to-market strategy includes targeting individuals actively learning languages and leveraging partnerships, such as collaborations with OpenAI for enhanced speech recognition capabilities.

## Funding & Investor Profile
Speak has raised over $150 million from prominent investors, including Accel, OpenAI Startup Fund, and Founders Fund. Notable individual investors include Sam Altman, Peter Thiel, and Lachy Groom. The company recently completed a $78 million Series C funding round at a $1 billion valuation.

## Growth Momentum
The company has demonstrated significant growth, achieving a valuation of $500 million in June 2024 and being recognized as Google Play's "Best App of 2024" in Hong Kong, Korea, and Taiwan. Speak has also expanded its offerings with features like Live Roleplays powered by OpenAI and the introduction of the Speak Tutor.

## Visibility & Market Sentiment
Speak has garnered positive user feedback, with a 4.8 rating and over 15 million downloads. Users appreciate the app's ability to provide detailed feedback and a personalized learning experience. The company states that its approach to language education is reimagining traditional methods, which resonates well with its audience.

## Risks and Challenges
Potential risks for Speak include competition from established language learning platforms and the need to continuously innovate to maintain user engagement. Additionally, reliance on AI technology may pose challenges related to accuracy and user experience.

## Outlook
The outlook for Speak appears positive, with ongoing expansion plans and a strong user base. The company's innovative approach to language learning, combined with significant funding and market recognition, positions it well for future growth.

## Company Overview
Baseten is a private AI startup focused on delivering products for engineering and AI teams to enhance their productivity. The company emphasizes reducing engineering time spent on infrastructure while providing a seamless experience for users.

## Business Model and GTM
Baseten offers a platform that includes dedicated deployments, model APIs, training, inference stacks, model runtimes, and multi-cloud capacity management. The company targets engineering and machine learning teams, providing solutions that abstract away the complexity of AI model deployments and MLOps.

## Growth Momentum
The company has received positive feedback from users, indicating significant improvements in productivity and efficiency. For instance, Baseten reportedly cut P95 latency by 80% for Superhuman's AI-native email app, showcasing its impact on performance.

## Visibility & Market Sentiment
The company states that it has been recognized as a clear winner among inference providers, with users expressing high satisfaction regarding the ease of use and the ability to quickly deploy machine learning projects.

## Company Overview
Notion is a private AI startup based in San Francisco, with additional offices around the world. The company aims to create a versatile workspace that allows users to manage their life and work using customizable software building blocks. Notion's mission is to make software tailored to individual problems a ubiquitous reality.

## Business Model and GTM
Notion operates on a subscription-based model, offering various plans for teams, enterprises, and individuals. The company provides a platform that integrates multiple functionalities, including project management, documentation, and AI-driven tools. The company states that it blends workflow into an all-in-one workspace, allowing users to customize their experience.

## Growth Momentum
Notion has over 100 million users worldwide and claims to be the #1 knowledge base for three consecutive years according to G2. The company also reports that 62% of Fortune 100 companies and over 50% of Y Combinator companies utilize its services. The company has a community of over 1.4 million members.

## Visibility & Market Sentiment
The company has received positive media coverage, being featured in outlets like Forbes and Fast Company. The company states that it is growing faster than ever and has partnerships with major brands like Samsung to enhance its offerings.

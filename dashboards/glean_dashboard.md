## Company Overview
Glean is an AI-powered work assistant designed to enhance productivity by providing employees with answers, generating content, and automating tasks. Founded by a team of former Google search engineers, Glean aims to transform the way employees interact with information at work. The company is headquartered at 260 Sheridan Ave, Suite 300, Palo Alto, CA 94306, United States.

## Business Model and GTM
Glean operates on a SaaS model, offering a Work AI platform that includes features such as enterprise search, data analysis, and AI agents. The company targets various industries, including retail, financial services, higher education, healthcare, and government. Glean's go-to-market strategy focuses on engaging customers through a combination of product innovation and customer support.

## Growth Momentum
Glean has been recognized as a leader in the AI space, being named #1 in Applied AI and ranked among the Top 6 Globally.

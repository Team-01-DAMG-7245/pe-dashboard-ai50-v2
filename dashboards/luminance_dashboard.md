## Company Overview
Luminance is a private AI startup founded in 2015 by mathematicians. The company specializes in Legal-Grade™ AI technology designed to automate, expedite, and enhance contract activity across various business functions. Luminance's platform is utilized by over 700 organizations in 70 countries, including major consultancy firms and global companies such as AMD, BBC Studios, Hitachi, Liberty Mutual, and Koch Industries.

## Business Model and GTM
Luminance operates on a B2B model, providing AI solutions that cater to legal compliance, procurement, finance, HR, sales, and marketing teams. The company emphasizes its ability to deliver value across any geography, industry, or business function. The company states that its AI technology automates and augments every touchpoint a business has with its contracts, including drafting, negotiating, analyzing, complying, investigating, and collaborating.

## Funding & Investor Profile
In early 2025, Luminance raised $75 million in Series C funding. The company is backed by world-leading investors, although specific investor names beyond March Capital are not disclosed.

## Growth Momentum
Luminance has been recognized for its rapid growth, being featured in Forbes’ AI 50 List and the Inc. 5000 Fastest-Growing Companies in America. The company claims to achieve significant time savings for its customers, including 90% time-savings on contract review and 98% reduction in contract management costs.

## Visibility & Market Sentiment
The company has received positive media coverage, with mentions in The Financial Times, TechCrunch, and The Times. Luminance has also been recognized for its innovative technology and was named Best AI Product for Legal at the CogX Awards. The company states that it is perceived as a leader in the legal AI space, with testimonials highlighting its effectiveness and comprehensive solutions.

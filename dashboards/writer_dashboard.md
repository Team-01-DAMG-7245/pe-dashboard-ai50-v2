## Company Overview
WRITER is an AI startup focused on transforming enterprise operations through innovative AI solutions. The company aims to compress the time between ideation and execution, enabling organizations to create high-quality insights and solutions that comply with brand, legal, and regulatory standards. Founded by May Habib and Waseem AlShikh, WRITER operates from multiple locations, including San Francisco, New York, London, Chicago, and Austin.

## Business Model and GTM
WRITER offers an end-to-end agent builder platform that unites IT and business teams, allowing them to build, activate, and supervise AI agents collaboratively. The platform includes various capabilities such as Action Agent, AI Studio, and Palmyra LLMs, designed to enhance productivity across departments like finance, HR, legal, marketing, sales, and support. The company targets industries including financial services, healthcare, retail, and technology.

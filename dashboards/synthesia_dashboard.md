## Company Overview
Synthesia Technology is a Spanish holding company specializing in the manufacturing and commercialization of chemical products, polyester polyols, and polyurethane systems for thermal and acoustic insulation, as well as various industrial applications. With over 50 years of experience, Synthesia is recognized as a leader in the residential and industrial insulation markets. The company is part of the Irish group Kingspan, known for its high-performance thermal insulation technologies.

## Business Model and GTM
Synthesia operates through several business units, including Polyurethane Systems, Polyester Polyols, Specialties, and Distribution of Raw Materials (Americas). The company emphasizes research, development, and innovation, positioning itself as one of the few independent companies with proprietary technology in the sector. Synthesia offers tailored solutions to meet the specific needs of its clients, focusing on service, availability, and application knowledge.

## Company Overview
Abridge is a clinician-led AI startup focused on transforming patient-clinician conversations into contextually aware, clinically useful, and billable AI-generated notes. The company aims to improve outcomes for clinicians, nurses, and revenue cycle teams at scale. Abridge's technology is integrated directly into existing electronic health record (EHR) workflows, particularly within Epic systems.

## Business Model and GTM
Abridge operates on an enterprise-grade AI model, providing solutions for clinical documentation, revenue cycle management, and nursing documentation. The company targets large healthcare systems and has established partnerships with notable organizations such as Johns Hopkins Medicine, Kaiser Permanente, Duke Health, and Mayo Clinic. The company states that its platform is designed to ease the cognitive load on clinicians and enhance their professional fulfillment.

## Company Overview
Hebbia is an AI startup focused on transforming the finance, legal, and consulting industries through innovative software solutions. The company has pioneered retrieval augmented generation (RAG) and has developed a proprietary architecture known as ISD, which allows for complex task handling. Hebbia envisions a world where a single, agentic interface can solve any knowledge problem.

## Business Model and GTM
Hebbia's business model revolves around providing AI solutions tailored for finance, law, and corporate sectors. The company claims to have over 1,000 live use cases that drive real business outcomes, enabling firms to leverage generative AI effectively from day one without the need for in-house development or fine-tuning.

## Funding & Investor Profile
Hebbia recently raised $130 million in a Series B funding round led by a16z. The company has received backing from notable investors including Eric Schmidt from Google Ventures and Radical Ventures.

## Growth Momentum
Since its inception in 2020, Hebbia has raised four rounds of financing and has onboarded over 5,000 AI agents. The company reports a total addressable market (TAM) of $14 trillion across firms utilizing its solutions.

## Visibility & Market Sentiment
The company states that it is the world expert in applied AI, pushing the limits of reasoning and human-computer interaction. Hebbia has garnered positive sentiment from industry leaders, with claims that it is "The Rolls Royce of AI."

## Outlook
Hebbia is positioned for continued growth as it expands its offerings and client base in the finance, legal, and consulting sectors. The company aims to maintain its leadership in AI innovation and application.

## Company Overview
StackBlitz is a private AI startup that offers instant development environments through its product, Bolt. The company aims to revolutionize how software is built by allowing users to create and launch applications directly from their browser. StackBlitz is powered by WebContainers, a WebAssembly-based micro operating system that enables fast and secure development environments.

## Business Model and GTM
StackBlitz operates on a product-based model, offering services such as Bolt.new, Enterprise Server, Integrations, Design Systems, WebContainer API, and Web Publisher Platform. The company targets product and engineering teams, particularly in frontend development, and emphasizes a one-click startup experience.

## Company Overview
Clay is a private AI startup based in New York, USA. Founded in 2017, Clay specializes in Sales Automation, Data Enrichment, AI Tools, Customer Relationship Management (CRM), and Go-To-Market Strategies. The company's active products include Claygent and GTM tools.

## Business Model and GTM
Clay provides AI-powered tools for business growth and data enrichment that help companies in their Go-To-Market strategies. The company offers three pricing tiers: Free, Pro, and Enterprise. Some of their notable customers include OpenAI, Vanta, Intercom, Ramp, Anthropic, and Verkada. The company states that it is focused on hiring in the areas of Engineering, Sales, Marketing, Product Design, Customer Experience, GTM, and People.

## Funding & Investor Profile
Clay has raised a total of $1.678 billion USD across multiple funding rounds. The most recent round, a Series C, raised $100 million at a valuation of $3.1 billion. Prior to that, Sequoia led a $1.5 billion tender offer. Other investors in the company include Meritech Capital, First Round, Box Group, and Boldstart.
